Game 2: Build a City

Objective:

Construct a city out of puzzle pieces.

Of building the ideal city, Calvino writes: "At times, all I need is a brief glimpse, an opening in the midst of an incongruous landscape, a glint of lights in the fog…[to] put together, piece by piece, the perfect city, made of fragments..." This game presents such an 'incongruous landscape' with scattered pieces. Can you build the ideal city from them?
