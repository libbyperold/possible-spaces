Game 4: Tarot City

Calvino was very interested in tarot cards. In fact, in 1979, he wrote The Castle of Crossed Destinies, a book whose narrative was inspired by 15th century tarot cards. While they are not explicitly mentioned in Invisible Cities, much of Polo and the Khan’s conversation is concerned with the future of cities. In several of the invisible cities, Polo visits sybils and oracles; he learns the fate of cities, as well as their ruin.

What does Calvino’s future city look like? Can it be foreseen? Learn the urban fate by flipping over the cards in this Tarot City.
